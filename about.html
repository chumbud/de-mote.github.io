a little about me

I'm an aspiring UI/UX designer who currently attends Rutgers University: New Brunswick. I currently work at the school's Marketing and Communications Office as a print and web designer. I've been practicing design since middle school in 2006. That was when, for my birthday, I got my first Wacom Intous3 Tablet and copy of Macromedia Studio 8 (Adobe's Creative Suite before it was the Creative Suite). After installing the software and playing around with all the programs I could, my interest in design and animation sparked. Through the years I passed through many different diciplines until my first year of college where I took some Computer Science classes and fell in love with programming. It was then that I realized that I could combine both to make an awesome stew. (See: Web Design)

Now I’m a student who is still trying to find his way through the world. My passion in life is to leave an everlasting impression with every work I create. I take solace in fine detail and precision, whether it be through programming or design. For the rest of my life, I hope to always practice what I love and create work I can always be proud of.
