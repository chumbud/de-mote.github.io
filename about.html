nice to meetcha, i'm pj!

I'm a designer from New Jersey who currently attends Rutgers University: New Brunswick. During my time here at Rutgers I've been studying Information Technology and Informatics, focusing on web design and user experience.

My start in design was back in 2006 during the start of my middle school career when I was gifted my first tablet--the drawing kind, not the touch kind. With that I also recieved a copy of Macromedia Studio 8 where I had been given the opportunity to learn Fireworks and Flash. I initially started with animation, making whatever I wanted and gave it life through motion. By the time I was in high school I had a large portfolio of animated pieces that I was proud of but now slightly embarrassed of in retrospect. During the dark ages of puberty, I moved into design, learning Indesign, Illustrator and Photoshop--trying to learn whatever I could just to have fun.

It wasn't until freshman year of college that I started learning code. I can't say it was the easiest transition for a designer, but once I got the hang of it just making an animated button on the first iteration of my portfolio gave me goosebumps. (Yea, actually) It was then that I learned that code could add several levels of depth to a design past visual identity.

Now in my senior year, plans are always changing and I'm still trying to find out what I want to do with the rest of my life both on a personal and career level. The one thing that never changes is that I love to create, whether it was with legos as a child or animation back in high school. My goal is to belong somewhere where I can have control over what I make and give it life through interactivity. Every detail is important to me and I strive to delivery work that I can be proud of.
