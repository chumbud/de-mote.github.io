    the anthologist

    A publication sponsored by Rutgers University. The Anthologist is the university's literary magazine that showcases students' poetry, prose, short stories and even artwork. It serves to give a platform to recognize and give an audience to students.

    I am responsible for the visual identity of their magazine. Around the end of every semester, the staff members and I come together to discuss the final pieces that will be in the magazine.
