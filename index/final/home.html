humble beginnings

HI-FI started in the heart of New Jersey in a small town called Freehold. Our founders John Smith and Tom Hardy have always had a passion for audio and design. Back in 2010 they paired up to utilize their skills to start up a small company that sells luxurious and high quality audio peripherals. be assured that our products will last an entire lifetime as well as never go out of style.

quality assurance

We take great pride in our products. we strive to make every step is taken to assure our customers will recieve the best and greatest product we can deliver.

With the strongest magnet system available on the market, our products provide the most precise and powerful experience possible. The 1.5 Tesla magnet system offers highly accurate imaging leading to pure, natural sound.
